### [The Sun](http://ca.netflix.com/Movie/The-Sun/70123489)


Rotten Tomatoes Rating: 91%

[![](http://cdn-0.nflximg.com/en_CA/boxshots/small/70123489.jpg)](http://ca.netflix.com/Movie/The-Sun/70123489)
From renowned director Aleksandr Sokurov comes a piercing historical drama about Japan's emperor Hirohito at the end of World War II. Faced with his country's defeat and the renunciation of his divine status, Hirohito must establish his own identity. Sokurov's exquisite cinematography accentuates this examination of diplomacy and personal responsibility as Hirohito agrees to meet with American Gen. MacArthur in an attempt to move Japan forward.
