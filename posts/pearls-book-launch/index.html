Pearls book launch

Tue Mar 4, 2008

On Friday I had the pleasure of attending the 27th Pearls book launch, the annual anthology of creative writing works by Douglas College students. My story, The Reluctant Teacher, was published in it this year. I’ve had some poetry published in Pearls before, but that was about 8 years ago, so it’s been a while. A few of the authors read by candlelight and I got to see a few familiar faces. Good times.
