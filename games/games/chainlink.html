How to play

Each new word must:
  • Be a real English word, 3–6 letters, no repeats within the word
  • Share at least one letter with the previous word
  • Introduce at least one new letter not yet in your chain
  • You have 3 lives. Three invalid moves and it's game over.
Score = unique letters introduced, plus bonuses for rare letters and streaks.
Press New game to start.
