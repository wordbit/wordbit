CHAINLINK

A turn-based word-chain strategy game. Light up the alphabet by chaining words that share letters — and try not to break the chain.

How to play
  • Start from a seed word. Each next word must share at least one letter with the previous.
  • Words must be 3–6 letters, real English (official Scrabble dictionary), no repeated letters within the word.
  • Introduce at least one new letter each move to fill the 26-letter grid.
  • You have 3 lives — three invalid moves ends the game.
  • You have 10 turns. Use them wisely — every submit (valid or not) counts as a turn. Skilled players finish the alphabet ~20–30% of the time.
  • Bonus: +5 points for each unused turn when you complete all 26 letters. Faster perfect runs score higher.
  • Score = new unique letters, plus bonuses for rare letters (Q X Z J K) and streaks.

Available Typing · real-time

TYPEDOWN

Real-time reflex typing. Words rain down from the sky — type them before they hit the ground, build combos, and survive the waves.

How to play
  • Type each falling word before it crosses the dashed ground line.
  • Each destroy bumps your combo; longer words and rare letters (Q X Z J K) score more.
  • Words come from the official Scrabble dictionary (NSWL2023) — expect the occasional obscure-but-valid word.
  • Missed words and mistypes fill the overheat meter — hit 100% and you lose points.
  • You have 3 lives. New wave every 12s with faster falls and denser spawns.
  • Special words (from wave 2+) appear with an emoji and dashed border — type them to trigger their power:
  • 💣 KABOOM clears every word on screen.   ❄️ CHILLZ freezes words for 5 seconds.   ⚡ RUSHX gives 2× score on your next 8 destroys.
  • Press Esc to pause.
