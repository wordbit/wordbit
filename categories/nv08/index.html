Northern Voice a wrap

Wed, Feb 27, 2008

All the clicking laptops around me were a bit daunting, but gave me something to aspire to. The morning kicked off with the keynote address by Matt Mullenweg, founder of Wordpress (the excellent blogging platform on which this website is based). Listen to the audio here. I then listened to a talk by the CEO of Broadband Mechanics, Marc Canter on bringing social to software.
