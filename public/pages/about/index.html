Stuart Rackham’s Blog

My name is Stuart and I’ve been programming computers professionally since the early Eighties.

This blog site is built by Hugo and is hosted on Github.

The look and feel is based on a theme adapted by Spencer Lyons from Jekyll’s Lanyon theme. More details in my Porting my blog to Hugo blog post.

I ported this blog from WordPress at the beginning of 2015, my old blog site can be found here.

I update my posts to fix errors; refine content; add additional content; delete outdated content or make changes because I’ve change my mind. I do not document the changes in the posts – you can see the changes I’ve made by looking at the Github repository.
